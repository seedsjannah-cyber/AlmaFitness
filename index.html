About Me

Hi, I'm Alma

I believe in you, even when you don't believe in yourself. I'm a certified wellness and life coach who's walked the path from self-doubt to self-mastery. I know what it's like to feel stuck, exhausted, and disconnected from your dreams — because I've been there.

But here's what I discovered: transformation isn't just possible, it's inevitable when you commit to yourself. FAQ

Everything you need to know about coaching and services

My approach is truly holistic, combining physical wellness with mindset and life coaching. I don't just give you a workout plan or diet — I help you transform your entire lifestyle by addressing the mental and emotional factors that impact your success. Every program is personalized to your unique goals, challenges, and lifestyle.

Online training sessions are conducted via video call (Zoom or similar platforms). You'll receive your workout plan in advance, and during our live session, I'll guide you through exercises, correct your form in real-time, and adjust the workout based on how you're feeling. All you need is a device with a camera and your workout space!

It depends on your goals and preferences. I can design programs that use minimal equipment (resistance bands, dumbbells) or bodyweight-only routines. If you have access to a gym, we can create more advanced programs. During our initial consultation, we'll discuss what equipment you have available.

Results vary based on your starting point and goals, but most clients notice improvements in energy and mindset within 2-3 weeks. Physical changes typically become visible after 6-8 weeks of consistent effort. Remember, we're building sustainable habits, not looking for quick fixes that don't last.

Packages vary based on your needs, but typically include: initial assessment and goal-setting session, personalized workout and/or nutrition plans, regular check-ins and progress tracking, access to me via messaging for support between sessions, and adjustments to your program as you progress. Life coaching packages also include mindset work, accountability, and strategic planning for personal growth.
